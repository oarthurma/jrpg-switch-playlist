What is a JRPG?

JRPG, or Japanese Role-Playing Game, is a genre of video game that originated in Japan and emphasizes story-driven gameplay, turn-based combat, and character progression. JRPGs often feature anime-inspired visuals, epic quests, and party-based mechanics where players control a group of heroes. Popular examples include Final Fantasy, Dragon Quest, and Persona.

What is the Nintendo Switch?

The Nintendo Switch is a hybrid video game console released by Nintendo in 2017. It can be played both as a handheld device and connected to a TV via a docking station. The original model features detachable Joy-Con controllers and access to a wide library of games through cartridges or digital downloads.
